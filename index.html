Why exactly ICT?

What motivates me?

I run on pure spite (and caffeine). Just joking, but my determination and my stubbornness get me through a lot of the things I do. Otherwise, ICT for me is a giant playground where I can express myself, experiment, innovate and grow. In addition to that, I would love to facilitate people's day-to-day lives and explore new opportunities in this ever-evolving sphere. I approach every task as a puzzle or a mystery because I love "logic-ing" my way through anything. The way I learn is by doing and by setting big goals (projects in many cases) for myself. I like to get my hands dirty and let my curiosity guide me. This is why I've always known that the ICT field is for me.
