Frequenly asked questions (and their answers step by step)

How to print a document from your laptop at HZ?

  1. Go to the myhznetpay website and log in with your details.
  2. Connect to the printers through PaperCut if you haven't already.
  3. Select the documents you would like to print and specify the details (color, size, position, 2-sided) in the options menu.
  4. Choose the HZ printer, scan your HZ card on the TouchID.
  5. Select the prinjobs you would like done.
  6. If you have enough credit in your card to complete the print, press "Print" and wait.
  7. Afterwards press "Stop" and log out of you account.

  5. After that choose "Scan and Send", "Scan to me", "Yes", "Start".
  6. Then press "Start Sending".
  7. When everything is done, stop the process and log out.

How can I buy something on the HZ web shop?

In order to park at the HZ parking lot you will need your HZ pass which you need to show at the barrier. Then you're free to park your car!
