Welcome to my introduction!

Me, briefly

Hi, my name is Darina Masureva, but all my friends at home call me Max (it's a long story). I'm 19 and I come from Bulgaria. I speak Bulgarian, English, French, un poquitito Espagnol and I'm starting to learn some basic Dutch through duolingo. Art and science are my life and I'm a big foodie. I thrive near the sea and I love my friends more than anything.

All of my ideas start on paper, which is why this is the theme of my portfolio website. I love doodling and sketching any and everywhere, so I hope you can experience a bit of what looking through my notebooks is like.

Passions and hobbies

My interests and hobbies are very diverse. They range from music (especially rock and metal) to art, to literature, to games, to biology, to quantum physics. I sometimes feel torn between art and science, but, at the end of the day, science is a form of art, too.

I am always ready to yap about any of my interests.
