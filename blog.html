My motivation and study choice activities

I attended webinars, contacted related agencies (apv, unify), contacted current students at the HZ, read a lot of forums, went through all of the information on the official site of the university. I chose this program because it combines everything I'm passionate about and gives me the opportunity to show my strengths and work on my weakenesses. This university has felt like a safe place to experiment and learn at my own pace from the start which I love.

After graduating...

In four years' time I hope to become a software engineer in the medical field because biology is also a passion of mine. My plan B is game development since I also consider myself to be a creative person and have experience with a lot of different creative media. No matter my final choice of profession I will try my best to take my teamworking and problem-solving skills to the next level as well as gain experience.
